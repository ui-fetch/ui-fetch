Force Measurements

Force measurements were done to determine how powerful the clipping motors need to be. Need to be able to cut at max a 1 inch diameter branch.

  • Red line indicates cutting point for consistency

  • Bar was marked every 5 cm
  • Weights were suspended from the bar

  • Each selected weight was moved outward along the intervals until the branch was cut

  • Ended up with a 1 liter and 0.5 liter bottle, resulting in 1.5 kg to cut a 1 inch branch

 

Microcontroller Tests

The following video demonstrates what the microcontroller is capable of. First it shows that the LED is controlled by a push-on/push-off button. Then it shows that the DC motor will either rotate clockwise, rotate counter-clockwise, or halt entirely, depending on the PWM signal coming from the transmitter. The motor will halt after rotating a certain amount in either direction, and when the transmitter is turned off it rotates fully in one direction, which when implemented will fully retract the arm if the signal is lost.
